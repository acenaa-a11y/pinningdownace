Travel & Accommodation

Getting Here

Everything you need to know about getting to Bangkok and staying comfortable.

✈️ Flights & Getting to the Hotel

Most international flights arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK), about 30 km from the Renaissance in the Ratchaprasong area.

By Taxi or Grab — The easiest option. Head to level 1 of the arrivals hall for the official taxi queue, or book a Grab/Bolt ride. Takes about 30–45 minutes depending on traffic, costing around 300–500 THB plus expressway tolls.

By Airport Rail Link + BTS Skytrain — A great traffic-free option!

✈️
Suvarnabhumi Airport → Airport Rail Link
Head to Basement Level (B1). Board the City Line towards Phaya Thai.
🚆
Ride to Phaya Thai Station
Trains run every 10–15 min, making 6 stops. About 30 minutes total.
45 THB (~$1.30)
🚶

Follow signs to BTS Sukhumvit Line. Short walk between platforms. Use a Rabbit card or buy a single-journey token.

Take BTS towards Kheha. 3 stops: Phaya Thai → Ratchathewi → Siam → Chit Lom.
25–33 THB~10 min
🏨

Exit BTS Chit Lom, follow signs. The hotel is a few minutes away.
~5 min walk

🚇 Getting Around Bangkok

Bangkok's BTS Skytrain and MRT are excellent. The Renaissance is near BTS Chit Lom and BTS Ratchadamri. Ride-hailing apps like Grab and Bolt work great. Taxis are affordable — just make sure they use the meter!

Bangkok Highlights

Our Favorite Spots

While you're in town, here are some places we love!

🍽️ Restaurants
🫓

Gaggan Anand

📍 ~2.5 km from Renaissance

Progressive Indian cuisine by a world-renowned chef. An unforgettable fine dining experience.

🍣

Sushi Masato

📍 ~4 km from Renaissance

Omakase at its finest — one of Bangkok's best-kept secrets for sushi lovers.

🇹🇭

Sorn

📍 ~5 km from Renaissance

Three Michelin-starred Southern Thai cuisine with rare, heirloom ingredients. Book well in advance!

🇹🇭

Nusara

📍 ~4 km from Renaissance

Chef Thongchai's elevated Thai tasting menu — bold flavors with a refined, modern touch.

🇹🇭

R-Haan

📍 ~6 km from Renaissance

Michelin-starred Royal Thai cuisine inspired by historic recipes and traditional techniques.

🍸

Rooftop Bars

📍 Various locations

Bangkok's skyline with a cocktail. Try Vertigo, Sky Bar, or Octave for stunning views.

🛕 Attractions
🛕

The Grand Palace

📍 ~5 km from Renaissance

A must-visit landmark showcasing stunning Thai architecture and the iconic Emerald Buddha.

🌿

Lumpini Park

📍 ~1 km from Renaissance

A peaceful oasis in the city. Great for a morning jog — just a short walk from the hotel!

🛶

Chao Phraya River

📍 ~5 km from Renaissance

Take a river cruise or ferry to see Bangkok from the water. Visit Wat Arun and Wat Pho along the way.

🙏

Wat Pho

📍 ~6 km from Renaissance

Home to the famous Reclining Buddha and the birthplace of traditional Thai massage.

🛍️ Shopping
🏬

Siam Paragon

📍 ~0.8 km from Renaissance

Luxury shopping, dining, and an aquarium. By BTS Siam — one stop from the hotel.

🏬

CentralWorld

📍 ~0.4 km from Renaissance

One of Southeast Asia's largest malls. Walkable from the Renaissance via Skywalk.

🏬

Central Embassy

📍 ~0.5 km from Renaissance

High-end boutiques and the stunning Open House library-café on the top floor.

🏬

Gaysorn Village

📍 ~0.2 km from Renaissance

Upscale lifestyle mall with luxury brands, right at BTS Chit Lom.

🛒

Chatuchak Weekend Market

📍 ~12 km from Renaissance

One of the world's largest outdoor markets. Perfect for souvenirs, street food, and adventure.

🏬

ICONSIAM

📍 ~7 km from Renaissance

Riverside mega-mall with an indoor floating market. BTS + Gold Line or free shuttle.
